Range Rules

At East End Rod And Gun Club, Safety Is Our Utmost Priority, And Our Commitment To Ensuring A Secure Shooting Environment Is Unwavering. Range Safety Rules Are Not Just Guidelines; They Are The Foundation Upon Which Our Club Thrives.

With Every Member And Guest Who Steps Onto Our Premises, We Emphasize The Significance Of Adhering To These Rules. Our Range Safety Protocols Are Meticulously Crafted To Safeguard The Well-Being Of All Individuals On The Firing Line, Fostering An Atmosphere Of Responsibility, Respect, And Professionalism.

Range Rules

All Firearms Brought Onto Premises Must Be Either Unloaded In A Gun Case Or Holder Or, If Loaded, In A Holster. Bellevue Gun Club Reserves The Right To Inspect Any Firearm/Ammunition On Our Premises. Bronze Members Must Bring A Gun And/Or Friend To Be Able To Rent A Firearm And Reserves The Right To Refuse The Rental Of Any Firearm As Deemed Necessary.

Everyone Using The Range Must Be 18 Years Old Or Older And Is Required To Know These Rules And To Sign A Liability Waiver. Anyone Under 18 Must Be Accompanied By His Or Her Legal Guardian Who Is Required To Supervise The Minor At All Times While The Minor Is On The Range., And To Sign A Liability Waiver With The Minor And On The Minorâ€™s Behalf.

No One Is Allowed Inside The Range Area Unless They Have Read These Range Rules, Signed The Liability Waiver, And Been Assigned A Lane By Bellevue Gun Club Staff. Do Not Change Lanes Without First Receiving A New Lane Assignment From Range Staff.

Ear And Eye Protection Is Required At All Times While On The Range. No Smoking, Eating, Drinking, Or Use Of Smokeless Tobacco Is Permitted On The Range. Low-Cut Tops And Open-Toed Shoes Are Prohibited On The Range. Wash Your Hands After Shooting To Remove Any Lead Dust.

Alcoholic Beverages, And/Or Legal, Or Illegal Drugs Are Prohibited On Bellevue Gun Club Property And Are Not To Be Used Prior To Arrival At The Range. We Have The Right To Refuse Service To Anyone. Anyone Who Has The Smell Of Alcohol Or Drugs On Their Breath Or Clothes, Or Who Appears To Be Impaired, Will Be Asked To Leave Bellevue Gun Club Property.

Only Two People Are Permitted In A Shooting Booth At A Time. Only One Loaded Gun Is Permitted In A Shooting Booth At A Time. No One Is Allowed Forward Of The Booth For Any Reason.
