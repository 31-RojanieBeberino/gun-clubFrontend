Refund/Cancellation Policies, Terms of Service

Refund and Cancellation Policy

Contributions, Gifts, Or Membership Dues Made/Paid To East End Rod & Gun Club Are Not Refundable Or Transferable, And Are Not Deductible As Charitable Contributions For Federal Income Tax Purposes. Payments For Events May Be Refunded If A Booking Is Canceled No Less Than 24 Hours Ahead Of The Scheduled Start Time.

Shipping/delivery policy

The Club Does Not Offer Any Shipping Or Delivery Services, Save For A Postage Mailing Fee When Members Opt Out Of Receiving Emails. This Fee Is Non-Refundable.

Terms of Service

See Our Standing “Hold Harmless Agreement”; This Must Be Agreed To Upon Joining Or Renewing Your Membership.
